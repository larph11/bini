Our Mission

At Bini, our mission is to spread joy and positivity through our music. We believe in the power of music to uplift spirits and bring people together. Through our unique blend of PPOP, we aim to create an inclusive community where fans can find solace, inspiration, and happiness.

Who we are?

BINI is a Filipino girl group under ABS-CBN's Star Magic that was formed through Star Hunt Academy, and is composed of eight members: Aiah, Colet, Maloi, Gwen, Stacey, Mikha, Jhoanna, and Sheena.
