Pantropiko


"Pantropiko" is the Pre-Album single of Filipino girl group BINI, and their second single released in 2023. It was released on November 17, 2023 at 12:00 am PHT on all digital streaming platforms. The song is also part of BINI's first Extended Play Talaarawan as its third track.

Salamin, Salamin


Salamin, Salamin or Mirror, Mirror in English is a fun and fresh bop that hopes to find one’s true love. Written by Mart Sam Emmanuel Olavides, John Michael Conchada, Paula Rose Alcasid and Paulo Miguel Rañeses of FlipMusic, it boasts of catchy lyrics and a colorful set design, perfect for that uniquely BINI vibe! Salamin, Salamin is part of their latest EP Talaarawan which includes their other incredible singles Karera and Pantropiko.

Karera


"Karera" is the Pre-Album single of Filipino girl group BINI, and their first single released by Star Music in 2023. It was released on September 22, 2023 at 12:00 mn PHT on all digital streaming platforms. The song was also part of BINI's first EP Taalarawan as its first track.

Lagi


"Lagi" is a single of Filipino girl group BINI, and their second single in 2022. It was released on June 24, 2022 at 12:00 am PHT on all digital streaming platform along with its official music video on Youtube. The song was also part of BINI's sophomore album Feel Good as its second track.

Golden Arrow


"Golden Arrow" is the fourth official single of BINI. It was released on October 14, 2021 at 07:00 pm PHT as the carrier single of their first album titled "Born To Win" The lyrics were written by singer-songwriter SAB aka Sabine Cerrado, with the rap lyrics written by BINI members Aiah Arceta, Mikha Lim, Stacey Sevilla and Colet Vergara. The recording of the song was done by Jonathan Manalo at The Purple Room while the mixing and mastering were done by Theo Martel. The vocal arrangement were done by Anna Achacoso-Graham & Jonathan Manalo.
